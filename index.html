Who We Are关于我们

Arwen
Borowiak Arwen
Borowiak

Founder & Head Coach · Based in Germany创始人与首席教练 · 驻德国

Born and raised in Germany, I discovered fencing as a child and it became my life. I trained across Europe — Germany, France, Italy, Poland, Denmark, Czech Republic — and later traveled to China and Japan, spending time at elite academies in both countries before competing at the highest international level across Asia and Europe.

我在德国出生长大，从小便与击剑结缘，视其为毕生追求。我先后在德国、法国、意大利、波兰、丹麦、捷克共和国接受训练，后来赴中国和日本，在两国精英学院学习，并在亚欧两大洲参加最高级别的国际赛事。

I earned a US university scholarship, became a 4× NCAA All-American, and claimed the US National Championship in 2018. In 2019, I won the European Championship and reached World #2 in the cadet ranking. Champion's Path exists to share these transformative experiences with the next generation of athletes.

我获得美国大学奖学金，四度荣获NCAA全美最佳称号，并于2018年夺得美国全国冠军。2019年，我赢得欧洲锦标赛冠军，在青年组世界排名中跻身第二。冠军之路正是为了将这些宝贵经历传递给下一代运动员而创立。
