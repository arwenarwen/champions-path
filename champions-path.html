Who We Are 关于我们

Arwen
Borowiak 阿尔文
博罗维亚克

Founder & Head Coach 创始人 & 首席教练

Born and raised in Germany, I discovered fencing as a child and it became my life's pursuit. I trained across Europe — in Germany, France, Italy, Poland, Denmark, and Czech Republic — before earning a scholarship to the United States, where I competed at the highest collegiate level.

我在德国出生长大，从小与击剑结缘，并将其视为毕生追求。我先后在德国、法国、意大利、波兰、丹麦和捷克共和国接受专业训练，随后赴美国留学，在大学阶段的比赛中达到了最高水平。

I have competed in Macao and trained at some of the world's most prestigious fencing academies. Now, through Champion's Path, I bring those same transformative experiences to talented young athletes from China — blending elite coaching, cultural immersion, and unforgettable adventures.

我曾在澳门参加国际赛事，并在世界上最负盛名的击剑学院接受训练。如今，通过"冠军之路"，我将这些宝贵的经历带给来自中国的优秀年轻运动员——将精英训练、文化体验与难忘冒险融为一体。

01
Germany · Europe 德国 · 欧洲
The German Classic 德国经典之旅
2–3 Weeks · Multiple Cities 2至3周 · 多个城市

Train at elite German fencing clubs in Berlin, Munich, and Frankfurt. Visit local schools and universities, attend a Bundesliga football match, and discover Germany's rich cultural heritage from castles to modern art. 在柏林、慕尼黑和法兰克福的精英击剑俱乐部接受训练。参观当地学校和大学，观看德甲足球比赛，探索德国从城堡到现代艺术的丰富文化遗产。

02
France · Europe 法国 · 欧洲
The French Élite 法国精英之旅
2–3 Weeks · Paris & Lyon 2至3周 · 巴黎 & 里昂

Train at prestigious Parisian fencing academies and Lyon's renowned clubs. Experience French culture, visit Sciences Po and top universities, explore the Louvre, and enjoy a Ligue 1 match in Lyon. 在巴黎享有盛誉的击剑学院和里昂著名俱乐部接受训练。体验法国文化，参观巴黎政治学院等顶尖大学，游览卢浮宫，并在里昂观看法甲比赛。

🤺 FENCING CAMP 🎨 LOUVRE 🎓 UNIVERSITY TOUR ⚽ LIGUE 1
03
Italy · Europe 意大利 · 欧洲
The Italian Masters 意大利大师之旅
2–3 Weeks · Rome & Milan 2至3周 · 罗马 & 米兰

Italy is a historic powerhouse of fencing. Train with Italian masters in Rome and Milan, visit the Colosseum and Vatican, tour top universities, and experience a Serie A match — football passion at its finest. 意大利是击剑领域的历史强国。在罗马和米兰与意大利大师共同训练，参观罗马斗兽场和梵蒂冈，游览顶尖大学，并观看意甲比赛——感受最纯粹的足球激情。

🤺 FENCING MASTERS 🏛 COLOSSEUM ⚽ SERIE A 🍕 CULTURE
04
USA · North America 美国 · 北美
The American Dream 美国梦想之旅
2–3 Weeks · Boston & Ivy League 2至3周 · 波士顿 & 常春藤

Train at NCAA-level programs in Boston. Visit Harvard, MIT, and Boston University campuses. Experience American college culture, a Red Sox or Celtics game, and the historic Freedom Trail — plus elite coaching from NCAA All-Americans. 在波士顿的NCAA级别项目中接受训练。参观哈佛、麻省理工和波士顿大学校园。体验美国大学文化，观看红袜队或凯尔特人队的比赛，游览历史自由之路——并接受NCAA全美最佳球员的精英指导。

Elite Fencing Training 精英击剑训练

Elite Fencing Training

Daily sessions led by Arwen and partnered local coaches at top-tier facilities. Technique, tactics, footwork, and sparring — for all levels from beginner to advanced competitor. 由阿尔文和合作当地教练在顶级设施每日带领训练。技术、战术、步法和对抗练习——适合从初学者到高级竞技运动员的各个水平。

🎓

School & University Visits 学校与大学参观

School & University Visits

Arranged visits to local schools and prestigious universities, with campus tours, student exchanges, and insight into European and American academic culture — invaluable for college-bound students. 安排参观当地学校和著名大学，包括校园游览、学生交流，以及对欧美学术文化的深入了解——对计划留学的学生极具价值。

🌍

Cultural Immersion 文化沉浸体验

Cultural Immersion

Sightseeing, local cuisine, museum visits, football matches, city exploration, and evening social events. We don't just visit places — we live them. 观光游览、品尝当地美食、参观博物馆、观看足球比赛、城市探索以及夜间社交活动。我们不只是到访一个地方——我们真正地融入其中。

🏠

Full-Service Logistics 全程无忧服务

Accommodation, ground transport, meals, and full supervision included. Parents can feel confident that their child is safe, supported, and thriving every step of the way. 包含住宿、地面交通、餐饮和全程监督。家长可以放心，孩子在每一步旅程中都安全、受到支持，并且茁壮成长。

🤝

Small Groups 小班教学

Groups are intentionally kept small to ensure personal attention, deeper friendships, and a higher quality experience for every participant. 我们特意保持小团队规模，以确保每位参与者都能获得个性化关注、建立更深厚的友谊，并享有更高品质的体验。

🌐

Bilingual Support 双语支持

Mandarin-speaking program coordinators are present throughout. Clear communication with parents and students — bridging language, culture, and sport. 全程配备普通话协调员。与家长和学生保持清晰沟通——架起语言、文化与体育之间的桥梁。
