Who We Are关于我们

Arwen
Borowiak

Founder & Head Coach · Based in Germany创始人及主教练 · 常驻德国

Born and raised in Germany, I came to fencing as a child and never stopped. I trained across Europe — Germany, France, Poland, Czech Republic — and later traveled to China and Japan to train at elite academies before competing at the highest international levels across Asia and Europe.

我在德国出生长大，自幼与击剑结缘，从未停止。我先后在德国、法国、波兰、捷克接受训练，后赴中国和日本在精英学院深造，并在亚欧两大洲参加最高级别的国际赛事。

I earned a US university scholarship, was named a 4× NCAA All-American, and won the US National Championship in 2018. In 2019, I became European Champion and ranked World #2 as a cadet. Champion's Path was born to share these transformative experiences with the next generation.

我获得美国大学奖学金，四度荣获NCAA全美最佳，并于2018年夺得美国全国冠军。2019年，我荣获欧洲冠军，青年组世界排名第二。冠军之路由此而生，旨在将这些宝贵经历传递给下一代。

My mission: bridge elite sport with global education. Every program is built on discipline, cultural awareness, and a belief that the best athletes are also the most curious citizens of the world.

我的使命：架起精英体育与全球教育之间的桥梁。每个项目都建立在纪律和文化意识的基础上，相信最优秀的运动员也是世界上最具好奇心的公民。
